India vs New Zealand, Champions Trophy Final: It was an all-round show for Rohit Sharma's men as India lifted the title, defeating New Zealand by four wickets.Rachin Ravindra gave New Zealand a quickfire start after captain Mitchell Santner opted to bat in Dubai. The Kiwis raced to 51/0 in seven overs, with Ravindra scoring 29 of those in just 21 balls.Varun Chakaravarthy gave India the opening breakthrough after a solid start for New Zealand, dismissing Will Young for 15 off 23 deliveries.
